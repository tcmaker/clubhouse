It looks like we've got all the information we need from you. There's only three things left to do.

Visit the Membership Desk

Your next stop is the membership desk, located in the classroom. Give the volunteers on duty your name, and they'll issue your keyfob.

Pay Your Dues

Out of concern for your digital privacy, we do not want you to enter your credit card or bank information into any of the publicly-available computers in our shop. Instead, we ask that you return home, sign into our membership dashboard with the account credentials you just created, and enter payment from the privacy and security of your own home. Your keyfob will activate when your payment goes through, and then you'll be able to come and go as you please.

Attend Orientation

We regularly hold orientation sessions for new members. It's a good way to get to know the shop managers and meet other new members. Plus, if you stay for Woodshop orientation, which is usually held on the same night, you'll get training and clearance to use our most powerful woodworking tools.
